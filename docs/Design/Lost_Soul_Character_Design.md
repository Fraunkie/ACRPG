### ‚öî **Abilities & Spells**

**Active:**

- **Spirit Burst** *(Level 1)*  
  *Deals AoE damage around the Lost Soul, healing self and allies for 25% of damage dealt.*
  - **Cooldown:** 30 seconds  
  - **Level Scaling:** Increases damage/heal by 5% per level.

- **Spirit Vortex** *(Level 5)*  
  *Summons 3 orbs around the Lost Soul, each orb can be fired individually. Each orb deals damage and heals based on 25% of the damage dealt. Orbs are used one at a time, and any unused orbs are reset upon the cooldown refresh.*
  - **Cooldown:** 20 seconds  
  - **Level Scaling:** Max orbs increase by 1 every 5 levels (Max 5 orbs at Level 30).

- **Soul Implosion** *(Level 15)*  
  *Deals massive AOE damage to enemies within range, and transfers a portion of damage to all nearby enemies as soul damage.*  
  - **Cooldown:** 60 seconds  
  - **Level Scaling:** Increases damage by 10% per level after the first.

---

**Passive:**

- **Spirit Resilience** *(Level 3)*  
  *Gain 10% bonus resistance to all damage types, increasing by 2% for every 5 levels.*

- **Phantom Echo** *(Level 10)*  
  *For every 10% health lost, Lost Soul gains 1% bonus damage to all abilities, lasting for 10 seconds after taking damage.*
  
- **Soul Bond** *(Level 20)*  
  *Nearby allies receive 5% of Lost Soul‚Äôs total healing power. This increases to 10% at level 25.*

### üí• **Talent Tree (Unlocked at Level 10)**

**Talent Points** will be allocated at levels 10, 15, 20, and 25. Each level will provide 1 point to spend. The final tree will be unlocked by level 30, opening up further paths.

#### **Example Talents:**

- **Path of the Revenant:**
  - **Soul Siphon** *(Level 10 Talent)*: *Increase healing received from Spirit Burst by 20%.*
  - **Unyielding Soul** *(Level 20 Talent)*: *Increase health by 10% after casting Spirit Vortex.*
  
- **Path of the Warden:**
  - **Guardian of the Lost** *(Level 15 Talent)*: *Nearby allies gain 5% damage reduction while within a 500 range of the Lost Soul.*
  - **Ancient Resilience** *(Level 25 Talent)*: *Spirit Resilience grants an additional 5% damage reduction on top of the current effect.*

- **Path of the Whisperer:**
  - **Mystic Surge** *(Level 10 Talent)*: *Spirit Vortex deals an additional 5% damage for each ally near the Lost Soul.*
  - **Soul Weave** *(Level 20 Talent)*: *Gain the ability to stack orbs for Spirit Vortex. Orbs can now stack up to 3 times.*

### üîë **Unlock Requirements**

- **Soul Energy Level** *(Main XP Level)*  
  - Unlocks new spells and talents as Soul Energy grows. Specific Soul Energy levels will allow for access to certain ascension spells, like **Eternal Flame** at Level 30.  
  - **Power Level** is used to determine general growth, but the majority of the abilities are unlocked by **Soul Energy** rather than raw power.

---

### üéÆ **Gameplay Tips for Lost Soul:**
1. **Early Game:** Focus on gaining Soul Energy quickly, and use Spirit Burst to clear out groups of enemies while healing up.  
2. **Mid Game:** Build up your talents and unlock **Spirit Vortex**. Use the orbs wisely for both offense and healing.  
3. **Late Game:** Prepare for **Eternal Flame** by focusing on maxing out Soul Energy and Power Level, readying yourself for the boss fight at level 30.
