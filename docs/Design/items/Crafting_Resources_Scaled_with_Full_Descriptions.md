# Crafting Resources (Scaled by Level with Descriptions)

This document contains a list of crafting resources categorized by skill level, with descriptions.

## Mining
- **Dragonstone Ore**: Level 1 - A rare, fiery ore that glows with an inner heat, often found in the heart of volcanic craters.
- **Chakra Crystals**: Level 10 - Radiating with pure chakra, these crystals hum with a gentle pulse, harvested from sacred caverns.
- **Digi-Metal**: Level 20 - A shimmering metal that flickers between digital and physical, found deep in the Digital Realm.
- **Mystic Slate**: Level 30 - A stone etched with ancient runes, known for its ability to absorb and store mystical energies.
- **Saiyan Core Fragments**: Level 40 - Fragments of the shattered core of a Saiyan planet, impossibly strong and full of latent power.
- **Electro-Sand**: Level 50 - Sparkling grains of sand that crackle with electric energy, found only during thunderstorms.
- **Bio-Metal**: Level 60 - A strange material that feels like living tissue, yet is as hard as steel, used for tech and armor.
- **Soulstone Shards**: Level 70 - Glowing shards that seem to hum with a whisper of lost souls, collected from the deepest rifts.
- **Vortex Gemstone**: Level 80 - A gemstone that shifts color as it captures the swirling chaos of the Convergence.
- **Neonite**: Level 90 - A glowing blue mineral that stores energy and releases it when disturbed, often used in advanced tech.
